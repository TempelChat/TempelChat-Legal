TempelChat Community Guidelines

Welcome to the TempelChat community! To keep our platform safe, respectful, and fun for everyone, please follow these guidelines:

1. Be Respectful

  • Treat others with kindness and courtesy.
  • No hate speech, discrimination, or harassment.
  • Respect differing opinions without attacking others.

2. Keep It Clean

  • No NSFW, violent, or obscene content.
  • Avoid excessive profanity or graphic language.
  • Use appropriate usernames and profile pictures.

3. No Spam or Self-Promotion

  • Don’t flood chats or promote unrelated content.
  • No unsolicited advertising, links, or promotions.
  • Use designated areas for promotion if available.

4. Protect Privacy

  • Do not share personal or private information (yours or others’).
  • Report any suspicious behavior immediately.

5. Follow the Law

  • No illegal content, discussions, or activity.
  • Report any illegal behavior to moderators or admins.

6. Moderation

  • Admins and moderators may take action to enforce these rules.
  • Respect their decisions and cooperate with enforcement.

By using TempelChat, you agree to follow these community guidelines. Let’s build a welcoming and respectful space together!
